Adam Martin - Web Developer

About Me

Hello and welcome to my portfolio. I'm Adam, and I'm thrilled to be embarking on this new career path of web development. But first, a little bit about myself. I was born and raised in South Carolina, but have lived in Kansas City, Missouri, as well as Osaka, Japan where I worked as an English teacher. I've spent the last six years in manufacturing, and while I have learned a lot during that time, I've begun to feel like it has grown stagnant and the opportunity for upward mobility has dwindled. While looking for my next career path I came upon the world of coding.

I was instantly intrigued at the variety of languages and their applications. I saw coding as a career field with almost limitless potential for growth. I began researching coding programs and bootcamps to find what would work for me. Then COVID happened. This ended up being a blessing in disguise because through COVID I found myslef enrolling in the Web Development bootcamp at Eleven Fifty Academy, a coding school in Indianapolis, Indiana. When so many schools were stopping enrollment they were taking everything online and opening their doors to students from all over the country.

Over the next twenty-four weeks I logged over five hundred hours in class and training in a curriculum that spanned HTML, CSS, Javascript, Node.JS, React, and Typescript. It also covered Github, Heroku and Firebase deployment, Postman, SQL Server, APIs, DOM manipulation, stateless compononents, session validation and more. Now I'm looking to further the knowledge and experience I've already gained to make myself a better, more experienced coder.
